## Licensing Overview

Business Central uses **named-user, subscription-based** licensing. Every user needs their own license; sharing is not permitted.

| Term | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Monthly** | Flexible cancellation; ~20% premium over annual pricing |
| **Annual** | Most common; locked in for 12 months; add users mid-term (prorated) |
| **Multi-year** | 36-month option; best price protection against future increases |

<Callout type="info" title="Price Update (Oct 2025)">
Essentials → $80/user/mo · Premium → $110/user/mo
</Callout>

## License Tiers

| Tier | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **Team Member** | $8/user/mo | Read-only access; managers & analysts who don't enter transactions |
| **Essentials** | $80/user/mo | SMBs (20–300 users); standard finance, sales, purchasing, inventory |
| **Premium** | $110/user/mo | Mid-market; adds full Manufacturing & Service Management |

All tiers include Power BI, basic Copilot, and AppSource app support. Premium AI Copilot features: **+$30/user/mo**.

| Purchasing & Payables | Full | Full |
| Inventory & Warehouse | Full | Full |
| Project Management | Full | Full |
| Human Resources* | Full | Full |
| Reporting & Analytics | Full | Full |
| Assembly Management | Yes | Yes |
| **Manufacturing** (MRP, BOMs, Routings) | No | Full |
| **Service Management** (Contracts, Loaner) | No | Full |

*No payroll — requires a third-party AppSource extension (ADP, Ceridian, etc.)

## Module Highlights

**Financial Management** — General ledger, AP/AR, bank reconciliation, fixed assets, budgeting, multi-currency, cost accounting. The foundation of every BC implementation.

**Sales & Service** — Quotes, orders, invoicing, returns, pricing rules. Premium adds service contracts, SLAs, and loaner management.

**Purchasing & Payables** — Vendor management, POs, three-way matching, approval workflows, electronic payments.

**Inventory & Warehouse** — Multi-location stock, serial/lot tracking, bin management, FIFO/weighted average/standard cost valuation, landed costs.

**Manufacturing** *(Premium only)* — Multi-level BOMs, routings, production orders, MRP/MPS, capacity planning, sub-contracting.

## Licensing Scenarios

**Distribution company (50 users)**  
→ Essentials: 50 × $80 + 10 Team Members × $8 = **$4,080/mo**

**Discrete manufacturer (30 users)**  
→ Premium: 30 × $110 + 20 Team Members × $8 = **$3,460/mo**

**Field service company (40 users)**  
→ Premium: 40 × $110 + 15 Team Members × $8 = **$4,520/mo**

<Callout type="warning" title="Exam Tip">
Manufacturing and Service Management are **Premium-only**. This is one of the most frequently tested licensing distinctions on MB-800.
</Callout>
